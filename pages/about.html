About SuhbatAI

Your Personal AI Interview Partner

Our Mission

SuhbatAI was created to help job seekers practice and improve their interview skills in a safe, supportive, and accessible environment. We believe that everyone deserves the chance to prepare effectively for their dream job.

Our AI-powered platform provides realistic interview simulations tailored to different professions, offering instant feedback and actionable recommendations to help you succeed.

The KasbSoft Team

SuhbatAI is developed by the KasbSoft student team at INHA University as part of our AI course curriculum. Our team is passionate about leveraging artificial intelligence to solve real-world problems and make a positive impact.

We're a diverse group of students combining skills in software development, AI/ML, and user experience design to create innovative solutions.

AI-Powered Interview Simulation

SuhbatAI uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze your interview responses and provide detailed feedback on communication skills, answer structure, confidence, and professional knowledge.

Our system evaluates your answers in real-time and generates personalized recommendations to help you perform better.

Educational Project

This is a student project created for educational purposes as part of the AI course at INHA University in Tashkent.

While we strive to provide valuable interview practice and feedback, please note that SuhbatAI is designed as a learning tool and not a replacement for real-world practice and professional career coaching.
